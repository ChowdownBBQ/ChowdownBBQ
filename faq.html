What smoker should I start with?

Pellet smokers like Z Grills are great for beginners — easy to use and consistent heat.

How do I keep meat from drying out?

Cook by internal temp, not time. Use a fast thermometer and let it rest before slicing.

Should I soak wood chips?

Nope. Wet chips steam. Use dry chunks for steadier, cleaner smoke.

Charcoal or pellets?

Charcoal = more control, more effort. Pellets = easier and consistent. Pick your style.

Do I need to clean my grill every time?

Yes. Burn it off hot, brush it down. Don’t cook in old grease.
