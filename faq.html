BBQ FAQ

Q: What smoker should I start with?

A: Pellet smokers like Z Grills are great for beginners. Easy to use, good flavor, fewer flare-ups.

Q: How do I keep my meat from drying out?

A: Use a meat thermometer. Don’t cook by feel or by time — cook by internal temp. Every pit’s different.

Q: Should I soak my wood chips?

A: Nope. It's a myth. Wet chips just steam. Use dry chunks for better smoke and control.

Q: Charcoal or pellets — which is better?

A: Depends on you. Charcoal = more control, more effort. Pellets = easier, more consistent.

Q: Do I need to clean my grill every time?

A: Hell yes. Burn it off, brush it down. You wouldn’t cook dinner in a dirty pan, would you?
