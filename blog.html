5 Mistakes Every New Pitmaster Makes

  • 🔥 Don’t skip preheating — your smoker needs time.
  • 🔥 Stop lifting the lid every 10 minutes — trust the process.
  • 🔥 Use a thermometer — guessing doesn’t work.
  • 🔥 Learn one meat at a time — don’t overload the grill.
  • 🔥 Clean your gear — it ain’t “seasoning” if it’s old grease.

What I Learned Cooking for 140 Sailors a Day

On a submarine, there’s no room for excuses. You make food fast, hot, and right. That work ethic? It built ChowdownBBQ. Prep is everything — don’t show up to your own cookout unprepared.

How to Handle Heat Like a Pro

BBQ is heat control: direct vs. indirect, hot zone vs. cool zone. Start with a 2-zone fire. Sear on one side, finish slow on the other. Use chunks, not chips.
